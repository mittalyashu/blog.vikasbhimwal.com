Din gye din aae.. 
Khusiyo ka khazana leker tum aae.. 
Na ummid thi aisi khusiyo ki..
Jin khusiyo me hum na aae..
Rishta na tha..
Rishta banaya gaya.. 
Samjh na thi..
Uss khuda se samjhaya gaya..

Palko se chaha tujhe..
Nindo se churaya tujhe.. 
Har dua me har ghadi..
Apna banaya tujhe..
